Method 2.A (Google Support on Kiosk Apps): 
1: Sign Out. 
2: Turn off Wifi. 
3: Go to Kiosk Apps is bottom left (TestNav, Fastbridge, BrowserTestSecure, etc.) 
4: When trying to connect to wifi, use the captive portal wifi. (only use your phone wifi) 
5: Click on sign in wifi. 
6: When the window opens, wait util the page loads or it says the connection was reset/lost. 
7: click on the web site info button. (the "i" button next to the url) 
8: click the help/learn more/google support link highlighted blue. 
9: click the waffle. (9 dots) 
10: Click Google. 

(If can't turn off wifi in sign in screen, Sign in and do #2 then #1.)

If can't connect to wifi in Kiosk Apps, Use this Method. 
Method 2.B (Google Support on): 
1: Sign In 
2: Use the Captive Portal Wifi. (phone, school guest, wifi guest, Mcdonalds, coffee shop, etc.) 
3: Do #5 - #10

Enjoy!
